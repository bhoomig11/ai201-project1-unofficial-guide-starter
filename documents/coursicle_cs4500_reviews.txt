--- GENERAL STUDENT ADVICE ---
- CS 4500 is a writing-intensive, project-heavy course — expect significant documentation requirements alongside code.
- Professor choice is critical: Matthias Felleisen and Jan Vitek have stronger reputations for rigorous but high-quality instruction.
- The course emphasizes real-world software development practices, team projects, and code review.
- Students going into SWE co-ops benefit greatly from this course if taken with an engaged professor.
