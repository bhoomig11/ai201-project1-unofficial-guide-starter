--- GENERAL STUDENT ADVICE ---
- Start assignments early — projects are long and time-consuming (15+ hours reported).
- Self-evaluation deadlines are strict — missing them results in a zero for the assignment.
- The course covers design patterns, inheritance, polymorphism, and UML — essential skills for co-op interviews.
- Professor choice matters significantly for this course — some professors are much more effective than others.
