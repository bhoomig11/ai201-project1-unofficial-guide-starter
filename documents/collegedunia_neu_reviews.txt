--- REPRESENTATIVE NEU MS CS STUDENT REVIEW THEMES (from Collegedunia) ---

Overall Program Rating: ~4.0 / 5 (based on verified student reviews)

Review Theme: Co-op Program Strength
"The co-op program at Northeastern is the best thing about this university. I completed two co-ops during my MS and received three job offers before graduation. The industry connections through co-op are unmatched by other universities I considered."
— MS CS student, Khoury College

Review Theme: Faculty Quality
"The faculty at Khoury College is a mix — some professors are brilliant researchers but struggle to explain concepts clearly in class. Others are excellent teachers. It really depends on the course and section you pick. Do your research on RMP before registering."
— MS CS student

Review Theme: Course Difficulty
"The MS CS curriculum at NEU is demanding but manageable with consistent effort. The workload is heavier than I expected. Courses like Algorithms and OOD require significant time investment. Scoring well is not at all difficult if you put in the effort, but you cannot coast."
— MS CS student

Review Theme: Career Outcomes
"After completing my MS CS at NEU with two co-ops, I had multiple offers from top-tier companies. The Boston tech ecosystem and the NEU alumni network are very helpful for job searches. The degree is well-respected in industry."
— MS CS graduate

Review Theme: Campus and Resources
"Boston is an expensive city, but NEU's location is central and gives access to a strong tech job market. The Khoury building has good facilities. The university could improve on housing support for graduate students."
— MS CS student

Review Theme: Course Registration Strategy
"Registering for the right professor is crucial at NEU. I made the mistake of picking a section based purely on timing. My friend took the same course with a different professor and had a completely different experience — easier grading, better lectures, more helpful TAs. Always check RMP and RateMyCourses before finalizing."
— MS CS student, Year 1

Review Theme: Algorithms Course Experience
"CS 5800 (Algorithms for the grad track) is the course that separates students who can handle the program from those who struggle. Some professors make it manageable; others assign weekly problem sets that take 10+ hours. The difficulty is heavily professor-dependent. Ask second-year students which section to take before you register."
— MS CS student

Review Theme: OOD / CS 5004 Experience
"CS 5004 (Object-Oriented Design for grad students) was the most practically useful course I took in my first semester. The projects were demanding but directly applicable to my co-op work. I used design patterns from that class in every internship I did."
— MS CS student, now at Amazon

Review Theme: TA Support
"The TA system at Khoury is a mixed bag. For popular courses like Algorithms and OOD, TAs hold regular office hours and are generally helpful. For smaller electives, TAs are harder to reach and office hours are infrequent. Go to office hours early in the semester before they get crowded."
— MS CS student

Review Theme: Networking and Industry Events
"Khoury hosts a lot of networking events and career fairs that are exclusive to CS students. I met my co-op manager at one of these events. Take advantage of them — they are one of the hidden benefits of the program that incoming students often overlook."
— MS CS graduate, co-op at HubSpot

Review Theme: International Student Experience
"As an international student, the co-op program was slightly more complicated because of work authorization requirements, but the Khoury co-op office was very helpful in navigating CPT paperwork. The program is very international-student-friendly overall."
— MS CS student, international
